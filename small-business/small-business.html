Anteraria ®

Hello there stranger. The goal of this game is to build a nest for the group of Ants below. Collect sticks and dirt to build your nest in the provided area on top of this little cave. Remember to breed your ants using the queen. If you don't have enough food, your ants will die. When a big enough nest is built the game will end.
